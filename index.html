About the Race

Experience one of the most anticipated F1 events of the year – the Horizon Grand Prix. Located in a scenic coastal region, the Horizon International Circuit is a state-of-the-art facility known for its high-speed straights and dramatic elevation changes. This year’s race features a brand-new chicane, more DRS zones, and advanced fan viewing decks.

The Horizon Grand Prix has quickly become a fan-favorite since its debut in 2019, attracting crowds from over 70 countries. The 5.9 km circuit will challenge the best drivers in the world across 57 laps of adrenaline-pumping action.

Fan Zone & Activities

Outside the racetrack, enjoy an immersive fan village with racing simulators, interactive pit stops, driver meet-and-greets, live music, food trucks, and more. It’s the perfect way to soak in the electric atmosphere of F1.

Travel & Accommodation

The Horizon Circuit is located 20 minutes from the city center. Shuttle services and ride-share zones are available throughout the weekend. Partner hotels offer special GP rates. Nearby airports: Horizon Intl (HZN), Metro City (MCX).

Media & Press

Accredited media can collect passes at the media center on Thursday from 9 AM. Press conferences are scheduled daily. High-res photos and press kits are available via the official media portal.
